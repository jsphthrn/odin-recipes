Tuna & Oysters Salad Sandwich

Description

Ever felt like having a light meal and then you think about a sandwich but the image of the common sandwich doesn't convince you? You'd prefer something with tastier than a classic sandwich and also a healthier option.

Very well, then. This Tuna Salad Sandwich should do the trick with added oysters just to add more protein. Adding wheat bread and fresh vegetables to the mix should now be more appealing. Go ahead and try this recipe right now.

Ingredients

  • 1 tuna fish can.
  • 1 celery rib.
  • 6 or 7 green onions.
  • Grinded sesame seed.
  • Celery seed powder.
  • Mayonnaise.
  • Mustard
  • Hot Sauce.
  • 1 smoked oysters can.
  • Any kind of bread. Wheat bread is suggested.
  • Pickles.

Steps

  1. Open the tuna fish can and drain it.
  2. Cut off the ends of the celery rib and make thin cuts through out all the celery.
  3. Cut off the ends of the green onion and, as the celery, make thin cuts through out all the green onion.
  4. Add the contents of the tuna fish can to a bowl or recipent.
  5. Grind the sesame seeds, adding it to the recipent.
  6. Add mayonnaise into the recipent as you like.
  7. Add mustard into the recipent as you like.
  8. Open the smoked oysters can. Wash off the oil from the contents of the can.
  9. Add the contents of the oysters into the recipent can as you like (suggested 2/3 of can contents).
  10. Mix the contents of the recipent until it seems all integrated.
  11. Take two pieces of bread and drop the contents of the recipent into a piece of bread (toasting the bread is suggested).
  12. Add pickles as you like.
  13. Enjoy!
