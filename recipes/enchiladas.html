Enchiladas

Description

Enchiladas are a very common dish in Mexico. Using chicken broth as a base, Enchiladas are, essentially, tacos with a sauce on the top of it. As simplistic as it sounds, Enchiladas are a complete meal and a pretty good recipe if you happen to like spicy meals... but is not like it is extremely spicy though so don't worry!

This meal should be enough to 4-5 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast (cut by the half and without skin is suggested).
  • 1 onion.
  • 1 garlic.
  • 1 kg of green tomatoes.
  • Salt.
  • Water.
  • Vegetal oil
  • Chicken broth powder
  • 2 Serrano peppers.
  • Acid cream
  • Corn tortillas.
  • Panela cheese.

Steps

  1. Into a pressure cooker put the chicken broth, a garlic clove, and half the onion.
  2. Add 1 and a half tablespoons of salt an pour water so the water level is about 3/4 of the pressure cooker capacity.
  3. Close the cooker and put in on the stove. Let it cook so the preassure valve starts making sounds.
  4. When the pressure cook starts making sounds, let it cook for another five minutes.
  5. Let it get a bit cold so the security lock opens.
  6. Wash the green tomatoes and put them into a blender along a garlic clove, half an onion and two serrano peppers.
  7. Pour into the blender part of the chicken broth from the pressure cook (using a strainer is adviced).
  8. Blend the mix.
  9. Into a stew pot, pour some vegetal oil and heat it up on the stove for 1 minute.
  10. Pour the blended mix into the stew pot.
  11. Add a cube of chicken broth powder into the stew pot. Mix it constantly so the powder disolves.
  12. Give a taste of this sauce and add water, chicken broth from the pressure cooker or salt as you like.
  13. Leave it in the heat of the stove until the sauce starts to boil. Then, leave it some minutes but don't leave it reduce the volume too much.
  14. Take out the chicken breast from the pressure cook and crumble it.
  15. Inside a frying pan, pour some vegetal oil, fry some tortillas, with out leaving them become rigid.
  16. In each tortilla put some crumbled chicken and then roll it so it makes a taco.
  17. Pour some sauce over the tacos.
  18. You can add acid cream, panela cheese or onion slices as you like.
  19. Enjoy!
